Welcome to CID
 
Effective people strategies are critical to companies' success. Ask managers about their greatest business challenge, and most respond with some form of people issue. Corporate Insights & Development (CID) assists managers in meeting their business goals by ensuring their people strategies support what they are trying to accomplish. Our services include: executive coaching, leadership training/ development, design & facilitation of management meetings, change management, organization structuring, pre-employment and development assessments, team building, 360° feedback, and designed implementation of high performance systems. We also engage in many special projects, including improving morale, developing relationships across work groups, reducing turnover, training, building management bench strength, strengthening corporate culture, and supporting other corporate initiatives, such as mergers & acquisitions or the shaping and introduction of major new programs.
